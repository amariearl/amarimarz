Dominating College Football

The Clemson Tigers represent Clemson University in the sport of American football. They are consistently ranked among the most elite college football programs.

Learn About Us

Since 1896, the program has over 700 wins and has achieved three consensus Division I Football National Championships in the modern era.With 25 total conference titles, Clemson is one of the founding members of the ACC, and holds 19 ACC titles, the most of any member, and holds the most combined conference football titles of any Atlantic Coast Conference school.
